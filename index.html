AI Awareness Blog

This website is created to spread awareness about Artificial Intelligence, its uses, advantages, disadvantages, and impact on daily life.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating smart machines capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision making.

Applications of AI

AI in Education

AI helps students through personalized learning, smart tutoring systems, and automated grading.

AI in Healthcare

AI assists doctors in disease diagnosis, medical imaging, and improving patient care.

AI in Daily Life

Examples include voice assistants, recommendation systems, and smart home devices.

Advantages of AI

  • Reduces human effort
  • Improves accuracy
  • Works 24/7
  • Fast decision making

Disadvantages of AI

  • Job displacement
  • High development cost
  • Over-dependence on machines
  • Lack of human emotions
