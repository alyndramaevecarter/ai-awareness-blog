What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines and computer systems to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

AI is widely used in technologies like voice assistants, recommendation systems, and smart applications.

Advantages and Disadvantages of AI

AI offers many advantages such as automation, increased accuracy, and the ability to work continuously without fatigue.

However, AI also has disadvantages including job displacement, high development costs, and over-dependence on machines.

AI in Education and Daily Life

In education, AI provides personalized learning, smart tutoring systems, and automated grading tools.

In daily life, AI is used in mobile phones, smart devices, online shopping recommendations, and navigation systems.
