What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of machines and computer systems to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

AI systems are used in everyday technologies such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and smart applications, making life easier and more efficient.

Advantages and Disadvantages of AI

Artificial Intelligence offers many advantages such as automation, increased accuracy, and the ability to work continuously without fatigue. AI helps businesses and individuals save time and improve productivity.

However, AI also has disadvantages. It may lead to job displacement, high development costs, and over-dependence on machines. Therefore, responsible and ethical use of AI is very important.

AI in Education and Daily Life

In education, AI provides personalized learning experiences, smart tutoring systems, and automated grading tools. Students can learn at their own pace with the help of AI-powered platforms.

In daily life, AI is used in mobile phones, smart home devices, online shopping recommendations, and navigation systems. AI continues to shape the way people interact with technology.
