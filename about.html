About AI Awareness Blog

Making Artificial Intelligence easy to understand for students and beginners.

Our Purpose

To spread awareness about Artificial Intelligence and explain its concepts in a simple and student-friendly way.

Our Aim

To help students understand how AI works, where it is used, and how it affects daily life and careers.

Our Objective

To provide blogs, examples, and videos that make AI learning easy, interesting, and practical.

Project Information

This website is developed as a 1st Semester university project. It is built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and GitHub Pages to demonstrate basic web development skills and teamwork.
